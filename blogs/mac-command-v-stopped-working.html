Leadership: lesson's learned from the bad.

  24 January, 2018

  Jogendra Singh

 MacOS, OS, OSX

It was Sunday of December 2017. I was doing my winter iOS Development Internship at Wheelstreet Inc, Banglore. In the sunday morning when i start working i found that my Command+V (Paste) is not working. It is horrible, isn’t it? We copy and paste all the time. If paste is not working then you have to type all the thing that you can copy that is not less than a bad dream. I usually don’t shut down my mac for 2 to 3 days; I always put it on Sleep mode. I thought this problem is happening because of this. I restart my mac but problem was still there.

Heading?
