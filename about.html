What's the story?

Thanks for finding my website! I'm Ryan and I am an aspiring entrepreneur seeking contract / freelance work on a part-time or temporary basis while I get my business off the ground. I am professional, reliable and have many adaptable skills that may suit your business needs; whether that is financial analysis, research and appraisal (listed or private), business plan writing, strategic input or a commercial perspective.

My career began in Mergers & Acquisitions at one of the Big4 before moving swiftly into the investment team of a leading, UK, long-term private equity investor - Equitix - where I worked on a number of transactions across a range of sectors (transport, healthcare, renewable energy, technology) and on investments of £10m to £1bn of equity.

My decision to start my own company comes from a long burning desire I have always had to own and build an idea from scratch that people believe in and are willing to part with their money for(!). I also can't wait to create an enjoyable working environment where people genuinely want to be involved because they also believe in the company and the team they work with.
