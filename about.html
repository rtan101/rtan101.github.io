What's the story?

Thanks for finding my website! I'm Ryan and I am an entrepreneur seeking contract / freelance work on a part-time or temporary basis while I get my business off the ground. I am professional, reliable and have many adaptable skills that may suit your business needs; whether that is financial analysis, research and appraisal (listed or private), business plan writing, strategic input or a commercial perspective.

My career began in Mergers & Acquisitions at one of the Big4 before moving swiftly into the investment team of a leading, UK, long-term private equity investor - Equitix - where I worked on a number of transactions across a range of sectors (transport, healthcare, renewable energy, technology) and on investments of £10m to £1bn of equity.

The decision to start my own business comes from a long burning desire I have always had to own and build an idea from scratch that people believe in and are willing to part with their money for(!). I also can't wait to create an enjoyable working environment where people genuinely want to be involved because they believe in the company and the team they work with.

If you would like to know more about how my services could fit your business needs or to request a copy of my CV, please email me using the button below or at rtanna.freelance@gmail.com. Some examples of previous and live projects are available on my projects page.

If you're just interested to see how it goes or want to read a mix of some lessons learnt along the way with some random clichés and thoughts thrown in, please check out my blogs. RT
