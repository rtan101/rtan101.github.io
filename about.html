I'm enterprising, I'm determined and I'm a little bit obsessive.

What's the story?

Thanks for finding my website and having enough interest to navigate to at least one other page! I'm Ryan, I used to be your typical City of London finance bloke with a hot-out-the-cleaner's suit, crisply (self-) ironed white shirts and freshly polished black shoes.

I was climbing the corporate ladder quite steadily, starting off in Mergers & Acquisitions at one of the Big4 and moving swiftly into the investment team of a long-term Private Equity fund, so it was all going pretty well. However, I have always wanted to have my own company. To build an idea from scratch that people believe in and actually pay money for(!) and to create a working environment where people genuinely want to be involved because they also believe in the company and the team they work with.

So, now that I've acquired enough experience to get going and have some ambitious ideas, I'm taking a turn off the beaten track to climb the start-up mountain.
