Just Do It

I'm restless, I'm obsessive and nothing excites me more than opportunity.

What's the story?

Thanks for finding my website and having enough interest to navigate to at least one other page! I'm Ryan, I used to be your typical City of London finance bloke with a hot-out-the-cleaner's suit, crisply (self-) ironed white shirts and mediocre (and occasionally) polished black shoes with a worn down heel.

I was climbing the corporate ladder quite steadily, starting off in Mergers & Acquisitions at one of the Big4 and moving swiftly into the investment team of a long-term Private Equity fund, so it was all going pretty well. However, (and I know it's a bit cliché but I'm going to use it anyway) all of that time I was climbing up the wrong mountain whilst seeing the mountain I really wanted to be climbing on the other side of the valley.

(I'm sticking with this cliché now so enjoy the ride...) The thing is, I did put myself on the first, well trodden path for a reason and that was to acquire the skills that would let me climb the unbeaten path I wanted to climb by myself later once I'd had the training.

And that's where I am now. I'm starting my climb up the new mountain, armed with some experience and ambitious ideas.

If you want to join in the journey please feel free to contact me. Or, if you just want to follow how it goes, please check out my blogs for random other clichés and thoughts. RT
