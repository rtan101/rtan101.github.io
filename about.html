Just Do It

I'm enterprising, I'm determined and I'm a little bit obsessive.

What's the story?

Jobs fill your pockets and adventures fill your soul. So I'm combining the two by splitting my time between consultancy services and working on new business ventures.

Recent contract work has involved advising corporates in the TMT sector on Mergers & Acquisitions, assisting other entrepreneurs with their short to medium term financing strategy, casting a fresh pair of eyes over various business plans, or delivering investment appraisals on companies for potential investors (both private and public).

My background is in transactions, having started my career in Mergers & Acquisitions at Ernst & Young before moving into the investment team of a leading, UK, long-term private equity investor.

I have a knack for finding efficiencies and use a combination of analysis, logic and resourcefulness in my approach. I believe that leadership is action, not position; and that emotional intelligence (in particular empathy) is the most important quality in making teams work.
