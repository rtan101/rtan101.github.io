ABOUT ME

I get excited about opportunity, I love being around people, and I would choose an experience over an object at any time of any day

Work Experience

Following university, I worked at a tech start-up called Unii Limited for a while where I worked on cool projects such as Fling, a project to design, build and launch an online student magazine, and to organise and take part in nation-wide marketing campaigns targetted at students.

I then started on the grad scheme at EY as an advisor on Mergers & Acquisitions in the energy sector where I worked on a number of buy-side and sell-side mandates.

Following EY I worked as an investor in the private equity fund Equitix where I invested long-term pension fund money into UK infrastructure assets, ranging from healthcare to transport, education and renewable energy and investing anything from £10m equity to over £1bn.

My passion has always been in entrepreneurship and now that I am armed with my accountancy and investment skills, I have taken the plunge to co-found two companies.
